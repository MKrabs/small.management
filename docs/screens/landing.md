# Screen: Landing Page

The public-facing entry point at the root URL. Minimal. The product explains itself once you're inside an activity.

## Concept

The page is a live, interactive demo of the slot editor loaded with a fictional activity ("Karaoke night 🎤 — 6 members waiting"). No marketing copy, no feature list, no screenshots. The visitor paints availability directly on the demo grid. The product demonstrates itself in under 30 seconds.

At the bottom: **"Want to do this with your friends? Create an activity →"**

The demo creates no real data.

## Secondary Actions

- "Join an activity" — small link or field to paste an activity URL
- "Log in" — for returning users with accounts

## What's Not Here

- Feature lists
- Pricing
- "Scheduling made simple" headlines
- Public browse of open activities
